Mark Scout — A Father in Season 3?

Mark’s grief has always been the emotional core of Severance, but Season 2’s finale cracked open a possibility that could reshape everything: Mark might be on the brink of becoming a father — and not just in his Outie life. This theory suggests that Mark and Helly’s growing connection isn’t just narrative chemistry; it’s foreshadowing.

If Season 3 explores the consequences of the severed and unsevered selves colliding, a pregnancy storyline would be the ultimate test of identity, autonomy, and what it means to be “whole.” A child between Mark and Helly would force Lumon — and the audience — to confront the ethics of severance in a deeply personal way.
