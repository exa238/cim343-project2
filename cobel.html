Harmony Cobel — Mark’s Mother in Disguise

File: Allentown

Cobel’s fixation on Mark has always been unsettling, but what if it’s not just corporate obsession? This theory proposes that Harmony Cobel might actually be Mark’s mother — a woman who severed herself long before the public knew the procedure existed.

Her eerie protectiveness, her emotional reactions to Mark’s grief, and her willingness to risk everything for him all point toward a connection that goes far beyond supervisor and employee. If she is his mother, her presence at Lumon takes on a tragic twist: she may be trying to shield him from the very machine she helped build.
