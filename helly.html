Helly R. — The End of Helena Eagan?

File: Tumwater

Helly’s Season 2 cliffhanger left fans buzzing with one huge question: is Helena Eagan still in control, or has Helly R. finally broken free? This theory argues that the Helena persona — the dutiful daughter, the corporate heir — may be on the verge of total collapse.

Some fans believe Helena might be “erased” entirely, whether through a botched reintegration, a deliberate act of rebellion, or Lumon’s own panic-driven intervention. If Helly R. survives while Helena fades, it would mark the first true death of an Eagan — not physical, but existential.
