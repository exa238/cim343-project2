Irving Bailiff — The Man With Too Many Secrets

Irv has always felt like the most disciplined, rule‑bound member of MDR… which is exactly why fans can’t shake the feeling that he’s hiding something enormous. This theory argues that his rigid habits and obsessive paintings aren’t quirks — they’re symptoms of a past Lumon doesn’t want him to remember.

Some fans believe Irv may have been part of a previous rebellion — one Lumon tried (and failed) to erase. If that’s true, his Innie’s awakening isn’t just personal; it’s the return of a threat Lumon thought it buried.
